help_outline Why should I design my app before developing it?

Designing your app prior to its development guarantees a streamlined and effective process that meets the needs of your users and business objectives. By planning ahead, you can save valuable time and resources, ultimately resulting in a more successful and user-centric product.

help_outline What is the difference between UI design and UX design?

UI (User Interface) design is concerned with the visual and interactive elements of a product while UX(User Experience) design is focused on the overall experience of the user as they interact with the product.
